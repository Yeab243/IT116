Neymar Junior

In May 2021, Neymar signed a contract extension that ties him to Paris Saint-Germain until summer 2025. In 2020, Neymar announced
an early exit from his contract with Nike's Jordan Brand and signed with Puma. In 2021, Epic Games
announced that Neymar, an avid video gamer, would join the cast of "Fortnite" with an in-game skin. His transfer from Barcelona
to PSG stands as the most expensive in the world at $263 million, which the French club paid in full ahead of his signing. His new contract
is packed with performance bonuses, including a massive bump if PSG wins the Champions League.
